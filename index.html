Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about OX-Bridge

Is OX-Bridge completely free?
Yes! OX-Bridge is 100% free for all Nigerian students. Sign up and access every feature immediately with no hidden charges.
Does it work for WAEC and JAMB preparation?
Absolutely. OX-Bridge has real WAEC and JAMB past questions with AI explanations. You can also generate unlimited practice quizzes on any topic.
Can I use it without internet?
Yes! Install OX-Bridge as a PWA app on your phone from Chrome. Once installed it works even with no internet connection.
What subjects does the AI tutor cover?
All 26 major Nigerian curriculum subjects including Mathematics, English, Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Economics, Government, Literature and more.
Is it suitable for primary school children?
Yes. OX-Bridge has a dedicated Kids Zone with fun games and learning activities designed for Nursery and Primary level students.
How is the AI tutor better than a textbook?
The AI gives instant personalized explanations at your level. You can ask follow-up questions, request simpler explanations, and it reads answers aloud — a textbook cannot do any of that.
